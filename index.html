Since 2024, I am expected to start master's degree in Artificial intelligence at Graduate school of AI, KAIST, co-advised by Prof. Kimin Lee and Prof. Jinwoo Shin.

My research goal is to develop AI agent following long-horizon human instruction in more human-like manner. Human can easily adopt to unseen environment and novel tasks, but current embodied AI agents or digital agents are not. To this end, I am focusing on various reinforcement learning problems (goal-conditioned RL, Hierarchical RL etc.,) and connecting them with foundation models (e.g., language models, vision-language models) which are capable of generalizable understanding of the environment and instruction.
