I am M.S student at Graduate school of AI, KAIST, co-advised by Prof. Kimin Lee and Prof. Jinwoo Shin.

My research goal is to make AI agent capable of understanding the world and sequentially makes decisions based on deliberate reasoning. For this goal, I am interested in aligning LLMs to possess behaviors necessary for decision-making (e.g., look ahead, backtracking, exploration), and also interested in finding or learning useful representations for sequential decision making. Currently, I am focusing on developing generalist agent for digital environment such as Web browser and Operating systems.
